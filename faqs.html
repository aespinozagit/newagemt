Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you except cash, card, or check?

    I accept cash and or card “only”, no checks please.

  • Do you accept tips?

    Yes, tips are greatly appreciated.

  • Will I be draped during entire session?

    Yes, you will be properly draped, the only part being worked on will be exposed during your massage session.

  • What parts of my body will be massaged?

    We will discuss the desired outcome of your session. This will determine which parts of your body require massage. A typical full-body session will include work on your back, arms, legs, feet, hands, head, neck and shoulders. You will not be touched on or near your genitals or breasts.

  • What’s the difference between massage therapy vs erotic or sensuous massage?

    Massage therapy - is given by a licensed professional focusing on manual manipulation of soft body tissues, such as muscles, connective tissue, tendons and ligaments, to enhance a person’s health and well-being. And is non-sexual.


    Erotic or sensuous massage - is the use of massage techniques by a person on another person’s erogenous zones to achieve or enhance their sexual excitation or arousal.


    Note: I have the right to end a session at any time if any inappropriate actions or verbal comments are used against me.

  • Must I be completely undressed?

    Usually during massage the client is unclothed. But it’s important you unclothe to your comfort level.

  • What if I’m running late, will I receive my full massage?

    No later than 10 minutes late you will be able to receive the full massage. After 10 minutes or more I will perform the session and end exactly when that session was intended to end.

  • How early should I arrive for my appointment?

    It’s best to be 10 to 15 minutes early due to the intake form if you are a new client and we can begin your session in a timely manner.

  • Have to cancel appointment?

    That is fine just try to give a 24 hour notice or call day of scheduled appointment. I can immediately reschedule your appointment to what best suits you.

  • What if during the massage you decide a different modality is what you need?

    I can change the massage right then and update your massage to its specific charges afterwards.

  • Do you have showers at your facility?

    Yes, we have a shower at our location. I recommend you bring a change of clothes and items such as blow-dryer, brush, shampoo etc.

  • Whats your Cancellation & No Show Policy?

    Please give a 24hr notice from scheduled appointment to cancel your massage. That time is exclusively reserved for you, and if you’re unable to keep appointment this will allow me to reserve time for another client. A text/call will be sent day before scheduled appointment to verify your massage. If appointment is confirmed the day before or day of and there is a no show you will be charged $35 on the day of scheduled appointment.
