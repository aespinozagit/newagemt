About Me

Hello, I’m Brianna Pacheco mother of two beautiful kids, and married now going on 13 beautiful years. I’ve been doing massage therapy for 5+ years. I love what I do. Massage itself is simply an amazing art form. Where I get to mentally and physically heal people. Touch is essential for us human beings. Its soothing and gives our muscles and mind relief from the repetitive and strenuous work we endure. It’s the natural form of medication and is super beneficial to our health. I love my clients! I love my work! And hope I continue to relax one body at a time.

About New Age

New age massage strives on teaching people the importance of touch. As we grow older we begin to experience less physical contact, while you might not notice the effects of not being touched right away, it can negatively affect your mental and physical state. Massage has tremendous Neuro hormonal effects on the body and brain. Cortisol decreases and serotonin and dopamine increase following massage therapy. With every session customized just for you. Massage will begin to mold and alleviate your pain and achy muscles. You will begin to understand the power of touch and the wonderful effects it has on your well-being.
